About
A lifetime looking
Art has been a constant thread throughout my life — from a school subject that genuinely lit me up, to something I've pursued seriously across decades, exploring different mediums before finding my home in acrylic painting.
My work focuses on landscapes painted from photography — my own images, or photographs shared by friends and family. A background in photography, including years developing black and white prints in a darkroom, shaped the way I see light, composition and atmosphere.
Having recently stepped back from work, I'm now dedicating proper time to my practice, taking on commissions, and learning alongside the Tadworth Art Group. I welcome commission enquiries and offer a selection of limited edition prints.
